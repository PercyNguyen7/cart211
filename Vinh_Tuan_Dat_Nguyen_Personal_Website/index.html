About me

Welcome, weary wanderer!

I'm Percy, and I am the sole creator of this website. You are right to assume that you have no power here.

I enjoy searching for the depths of the human nature. I love learning about people's past and understanding the choices that make. Therefore, please do not fret when I stare down your soul and ask questions such as "What is your mother's maiden name?" or "What is the name of the street you grew up on?"
