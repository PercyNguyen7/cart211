Homelessness

Style Guide

Inspiration

While the essay delves into facts, statistics and studies about the issue, the design of this webpage is inspired by my own interpretation of homelessness. I imagine one of the toughest challenges for the unfortunate individuals affected must be the night, when one is deprived of the comfort of home. No lights, no privacy, no security. Exposed to the cold weather or the dust and noise of cars passing by. The final website's design mirrors the life of a homeless after sunset. The user must navigate around the dark to find informations from the website, similar to how the homeless seek for a temporary place of rest in the obscurity of the night.
