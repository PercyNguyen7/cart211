Homelessness

Style Guide

Inspiration

While the essay delves into facts, statistics and studies about the issue, the design of this webpage is inspired by my own interpretation of homelessness. I imagine one of the toughest challenges for those without a shelter is the night, when one is deprived of the comfort of home. No lights, no privacy nor security. Exposed to bad weathers, dust, noise, cars and the cold glances of passersby. To be invisible to the public. To be uncertain of what happens next.

The final website's design mirrors the life of the homeless after sunset. The user must navigate through the dark to acquire informations, similar to how a homeless seeks for a place of rest in the obscurity of the night.
